About WeppCloud

WeppCloud is an online interface for running WEPP (Water Erosion Prediction Project) Model. WEPP is a physically based erosion model built to replace the Universal Soil Loss Equation (USLE) model. The interface simplifies the acquisition and preparation of topography, soil, management, and climate inputs for WEPP.
